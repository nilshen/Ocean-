Welcome to the World Under the Sea!

Spongebob's Birthday is in 2 days, move your MOUSE around the screen to recycle any GARBAGES that are throwed into the Sea! You will score 1 point each for the item you recycled but stay away from the SHARKS because you only have 3 lives. At the meantime, try not to let the GARBAGES hit the SHARKS because they are also part of the ecosystem!
